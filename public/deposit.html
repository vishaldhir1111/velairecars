Reservation and security

Deposits, holds and release

Velaire may require a reservation deposit, security deposit or payment pre-authorisation before a vehicle is confirmed or released. The amount can vary by vehicle, booking value, driver profile, handover location and insurance conditions.

Reservation deposits

A reservation deposit may secure a booking request while Velaire confirms availability, eligibility, handover logistics and payment status. A booking remains subject to final approval until all operational checks are complete.

Security holds

A separate security deposit or pre-authorisation may be required to cover excess, damage, late return, fuel or charging misuse, fines, tolls, congestion charges, extra mileage and other rental liabilities.

Release timing

Deposit release depends on vehicle return checks, payment provider processing and whether any charges, fines, damage reports or disputes remain open. Bank processing times can vary after Velaire releases an approved hold.

Deductions

Deductions may apply for damage, lost items, smoking or cleaning charges, late return, excess mileage, parking or traffic penalties, unpaid road charges, insurance excess or breach of rental terms.

Secure processing

Velaire does not store raw card details. Deposit and checkout payments are intended to be processed through secure providers such as Stripe, with Velaire storing only the operational payment state and provider references needed to manage the booking.
