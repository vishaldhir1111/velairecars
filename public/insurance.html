Insurance guidance

Eligibility and cover

Insurance approval is not automatic. Velaire may request driving history, licence information, proof of address, identity checks and payment verification before confirming cover.

Excess and deposits

Premium vehicles may carry a higher insurance excess and security deposit. The client is responsible for the agreed excess, damage, loss, theft exposure, fines and charges where applicable.

Approved drivers only

Only approved named drivers may operate the vehicle. Allowing an unapproved driver to use the vehicle may invalidate cover and create full client liability.

Excluded activity

Insurance does not cover prohibited use such as racing, track use, drifting, reckless driving, off-road use, unlawful activity, sub-hire, courier work or ride-hailing.

Incidents

Any incident, damage, theft, warning light, tyre issue or police stop must be reported to Velaire immediately. The vehicle must not continue to be driven if unsafe.
