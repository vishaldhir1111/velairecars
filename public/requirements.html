Before handover

What clients need

To confirm a rental, Velaire may require identity verification, a valid driving licence, proof of address, payment approval and insurance eligibility checks.

Typical documents

  • Valid full driving licence.
  • Proof of address, such as utility bill or bank statement.
  • Selfie or identity image for verification.
  • Payment method for deposit, balance or pre-authorisation.

Age and licence history

Minimum driver age and licence history can vary by vehicle. High-performance and exotic vehicles may require higher age, cleaner driving history and additional approval.

Handover conditions

Delivery locations must be safe, legal and accessible. Velaire may refuse handover if the location, driver, documents or payment status cannot be verified.

During the rental

Clients must follow mileage, fuel or charging, care, parking, toll and return instructions. Any issue with the vehicle must be reported immediately.
