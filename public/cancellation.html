Cancellation guidance

Booking changes and refunds

Cancellation terms depend on the vehicle, booking value, notice period, preparation work, delivery arrangements and any third-party costs already committed.

Pending bookings

Pending bookings can usually be changed before final approval, subject to vehicle availability and eligibility checks.

Confirmed bookings

Once a booking is confirmed, cancellation may affect the deposit or reservation fee, especially where the vehicle has been blocked, prepared, insured or scheduled for delivery.

Late cancellation

Short-notice cancellation may result in partial or full loss of a reservation fee where Velaire has rejected other bookings, arranged logistics or prepared the vehicle.

Velaire cancellation

Velaire may cancel or reschedule a booking if documents fail verification, payment is incomplete, the driver is ineligible, unsafe handover conditions apply or the vehicle becomes unavailable for reasons outside normal control.

Refund handling

Approved refunds are returned through the original payment route where possible. Timing depends on the payment provider and bank processing.
