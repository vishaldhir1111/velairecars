Client agreement

Premium rental terms

A booking request is not fully confirmed until Velaire has approved vehicle availability, driver eligibility, deposit status and handover details. We may decline a booking if the driver, payment method, documents, location or intended use does not meet our standards.

Bookings and availability

Vehicle availability is checked against blocked dates, pending holds and confirmed bookings. A pending booking may temporarily hold the vehicle while the concierge team reviews documents, payment status and handover logistics.

Deposits and payment

Velaire may require a reservation deposit, security deposit or pre-authorisation before release. Card details are processed by secure payment providers such as Stripe and are not stored directly by Velaire Cars.

Vehicle handover

Handover depends on safe access, accurate address details and client identity checks. The driver must be present unless Velaire has approved an alternative in writing.

Client responsibility

The renter is responsible for fines, penalties, tolls, congestion charges, fuel or charging misuse, damage, excess mileage and any use outside approved rental terms.

Prohibited use

Vehicles must not be used for racing, drifting, track days, commercial carriage, ride-hailing, sub-hire, off-road driving, unlawful activity or any purpose not approved by Velaire.
