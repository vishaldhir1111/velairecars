Optional Velaire Cars studio media slots

The active fleet presentation uses premium vehicle images from this folder until exact
GLB/GLTF models are added in public/models. To replace a vehicle image, upload a legally usable
asset with the same filename:

- studio-tesla-model-3-performance-2020.png
- studio-lamborghini-urus-2021-orange.png
- studio-range-rover-sport-svr-2021.png
- studio-bmw-m440i-convertible-2022-sky-blue.png
- studio-bmw-m140i-shadow-edition-2019.png

The active image and model paths are defined in src/data/fleet.js and mirrored in flow.js.
