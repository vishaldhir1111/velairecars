Data handling

How client data is used

We use booking and account data to confirm eligibility, manage reservations, arrange handover, prevent fraud, process deposits, respond to concierge requests and maintain a safe rental operation.

Information collected

We may collect contact details, booking dates, delivery locations, driving licence details, verification documents, payment status, vehicle preferences and concierge enquiry history.

Payments

Raw card details are not stored by Velaire. Secure payment providers process deposit and checkout details, and Velaire stores only operational status and provider references.

Document checks

Verification documents may be reviewed to confirm identity, licence eligibility, insurance conditions and fraud prevention checks before vehicle release.

Retention

Booking and verification records may be retained where needed for legal compliance, accounting, claims, insurance, dispute handling and fraud prevention.

Client requests

Clients may request correction, deletion or access to personal data where applicable. Some records may need to be retained for legal, payment, insurance or operational reasons.
