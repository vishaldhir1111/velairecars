Privacy policy.

How Velaire Cars handles the information needed to manage premium reservations, payments and concierge communication.

Last updated: May 2026 UK GDPR ready structure

1. Information we collect

We collect the information needed to review and manage a booking, including name, email address, phone number, billing and handover details, selected vehicle, dates, payment status, booking status and customer messages.

Where required for a confirmed hire, we may request driving licence details, identity evidence, proof of address and verification documents.

2. How we use information

We use customer information to process reservation requests, take secure deposits, confirm availability, complete eligibility checks, arrange handover, send transactional emails, support customers and maintain operational records.

3. Payments and cards

Velaire does not store raw card details. Deposits and payments are handled by Stripe-hosted checkout. We store booking and payment status so the reservation, confirmation and operations portal stay in sync.

4. Service providers

We may use trusted providers for hosting, data storage, payment processing, email delivery, fraud prevention, operational support and insurance or eligibility checks. Information may also be shared where required by law or to respond to chargebacks, fines, damage claims or safety issues.

5. Retention and rights

We keep booking and operational records only for as long as needed for the relevant booking, accounting, legal, insurance, fraud prevention and customer support purposes. Customers can contact Velaire to request access, correction or deletion where applicable.

6. Official guidance

UK data protection guidance expects privacy information to be clear and accessible. For reference, see the ICO guidance on the right to be informed and GOV.UK data protection overview.

Contact

For privacy requests, use the contact details shown on your booking confirmation or contact Velaire Cars through the website.
